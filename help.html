How do I export my productivity analytics? expand_more
Analytics can be exported via the "Analytics" tab in your main dashboard. We support CSV, JSON, and PDF formats. For automated reports, navigate to Settings > Reporting.
Can I integrate NeonTask with my custom CI/CD? expand_more
Yes, through our specialized webhooks. You can trigger tasks or status updates directly from your deployment pipelines. See the API & Integrations guide for payload examples.
What happens if I reach my task limit? expand_more
On the Pro tier, tasks are unlimited. Free accounts are capped at 500 active tasks. You can archive older tasks to free up capacity or upgrade to Unlimited at any time.
